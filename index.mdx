Welcome to the Fire242 Backend documentation. Fire242 is a Laravel-powered REST API that helps campaign teams manage voter registries, conduct field surveys, track voter card activity, and generate election-day reports — all under a role-based access model designed for Admin, Manager, and field User workflows.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Call `POST /api/auth/login` with your credentials to receive a JWT bearer token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore voters">
    Use `GET /api/admin/voters` (Admin) or `GET /api/user/voters-list` (User) to retrieve the voter registry with filtering and pagination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct a survey">
    Submit a completed survey for a voter with `POST /api/user/surveys`, capturing party preference, demographics, and contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pull reports">
    Generate constituency reports and voter card breakdowns via the admin reporting endpoints to monitor campaign progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All endpoints except `POST /api/auth/login` and `POST /api/auth/register` require a valid JWT bearer token in the `Authorization` header.
</Note>
