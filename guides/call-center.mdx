## Overview

The call center module gives field teams a structured queue of voters to contact. Admins and Managers can view the full queue, update contact status, and record the outcome of each interaction. Field users (role: `User`) can access their assigned portion of the list.

Voters in the call center queue are linked to the main `voters` table, giving canvassers access to registration details, constituency, and survey history during outreach.

---

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Load the queue">
    Fetch the list of voters assigned to the call center. Results are paginated and can be filtered by constituency, polling station, or other voter attributes depending on the caller's role.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact the voter">
    Canvassers work through the list, contacting each voter by phone or in person. If needed, look up a specific voter by their call center record ID.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update call status">
    After attempting contact, update the call center record with the outcome — whether the voter was reached, the conversation notes, or that a follow-up is required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Admin endpoints

All admin call center endpoints are under the `/api/admin/` prefix and require the `Admin` role.

### List call center voters

```
GET /api/admin/call-center/list
```

Returns the paginated call center queue. Supports filter parameters for constituency and polling station matching the voter attributes on file.

### Get a single voter

```
GET /api/admin/call-center/{id}
```

Returns the call center record for a specific voter by their internal ID.

### Update call center status

```
GET /api/admin/call-center/update_status
```

Updates the overall call center status. Used to mark batches or toggle the active state of the outreach queue.

### Update a call center record

```
POST /api/admin/update-call-center/{id}
```

Updates the contact record for a specific voter. Use this to log the outcome of a call — whether the voter was reached, their response, and any follow-up notes.

---

## Manager endpoints

Managers have equivalent access to the call center under the `/api/manager/` prefix:

| Endpoint | Description |
|---|---|
| `GET /api/manager/call-center/list` | List call center voters |
| `GET /api/manager/call-center/{id}` | Get a specific voter's record |
| `GET /api/manager/call-center/update_status` | Update call center status |
| `POST /api/manager/update-call-center/{id}` | Update a call center record |

---

## User endpoint

Field users can access the portion of the call center list scoped to their assigned constituencies:

```
GET /api/user/call-center/list
```

---

## State lifecycle

A typical call center record moves through these states during an outreach cycle:

| State | Description |
|---|---|
| Queued | Voter has been added to the call center but not yet contacted |
| Contacted | A contact attempt has been made and the record has been updated |
| Follow-up | Contact was attempted but the voter needs to be reached again |
| Completed | Outreach is complete for this voter |

<Info>
  The call center list and voter records share the underlying `voters` table. Changes to a voter's constituency or registration details will be reflected immediately when the call center list is refreshed.
</Info>
